COMPANY DETAILS

In the year 2020 Students of Vignan's Institute Of Information Technology, Duvvada got direct campus placements in top companies like Wipro, TCS, IBM, Tech Mahindra, Axis Bank and more.

Students of Vignan's Institute Of Information Technology, Duvvada received Highest Package offer of Rs. 5 Lakhs per annum and Average Package of Rs. 1.32 Lakhs per annum. Following are some of the best reputated companies that have visited for an On-Campus Drive.

All students have to complete a 100-hour English proficiency course that helps them to develop effective communication skills corresponding to industry standards. Students, from the 2nd year onwards, start receiving training for skill enhancement that ultimately helps them find good jobs.

The training session lasts for 200 hours spread over several days where students fine-tune their teamwork skills, soft skills, leadership qualities, analytical, and problem solving skills. Students also have to undergo a six-month internship program where they get a chance to apply their learned skills. Some of the recognized companies where Vignan University students have found jobs include CVK, Coca-Cola, HCL Technologies, Eureka Forbes, Arabian MEP Contracting, Merlin Solutions, and many others. The highest package offered to a student of the university has been Rs. 5 lac per annum. The average package stands at Rs. 1.5 lac per annum.
